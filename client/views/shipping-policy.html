Shipping Policy

Cosmos Shipping Policy

We all know that to ship something from one place to other, there is some cost associated with it which can be considered in two ways.

  1. By including shipping cost in the product cost.
  2. By charging shipping cost on and above material cost.

Cosmos follows the later policy to exclude shipping cost from the product cost to bring in transparency and eliminate the risk of including higher average shipping cost across the product portfolio.

We believe in delivering good quality product at the right time as it is shipped from our warehouses. To ensure timely delivery and proper handling of shipped orders, we have partnered with trustworthy and quality focused forwarders which are Blue Dart and Gati Express. For higher order quantities, we will facilitate our own logistic solution.

Shipping Time

We will ship the order within 2-3 days of receiving the order and it will be delivered within 2-10 days after shipment
Depending up on the delivery location. All shipping details and tracking numbers will be updated via email.

Shipping Cost Structure

Shipping Cost = Basic Shipping Cost (as per order qty) + Service Tax + Handling Fee
Shipping cost is subjected to change to capture change in fuel prices and other service cost
