Payment & shipping


Shipping



The cost and delivery time of your order are estimated online at the checkout.


The estimation is performed according to the weight of items and their destination.


We deliver orders at most of the places in India.

Shipping methods


Our courier partners delivers your shipment to your doorstep.


You can also pick up your shipment from our warehouse facility centres based at Noida.
This facility saves time and shipment cost for customers around Delhi-NCR.


PAYMENT


Cashless payment (invoices). This method is best for organizations. Place an order, print
the received invoice, pay it, and contact us to know the order delivery date.


Make a Payment through Neft / RTGS. When you place an order on Cosmos International.



 

Pay with a Razor Pay when you place an order on Cosmos International. Payment gateway charges may applicable.
